Twitter adds a tool to report spam and impersonation

Twitter ▪ Community Guidelines ▪ March 29, 2022

By Mathilde Saliou, a third-party contributor

To report tweets that are considered spam or impersonation, users previously had to go to the tweet and then use the “report tweet” feature. Now they can use a form without having to have a Twitter account.
