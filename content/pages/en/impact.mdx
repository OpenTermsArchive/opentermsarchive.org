Beyond immediate practical usage as a repository of historical terms of service, Open Terms Archive is designed as a tool to steer internet and platform governance towards increased accountability, improved legislation and stronger regulatory compliance.

## Recognising current limits

Online services benefit from relatively flexible regulatory regimes compared to many other industries, through legislations such as Section 230 in the USA or the e-Commerce Directice in the EU. These regulatory landscapes have made it possible for large, global services to emerge. These services have enabled a level of public discourse and information sharing never seen before.
