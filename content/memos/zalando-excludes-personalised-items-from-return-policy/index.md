Zalando [excluded](https://github.com/openTermsArchive/vlopses-ie-versions/commit/0b1ab976072bdac6607de592acab7ded37932d85) custom-made items from its returns and cancellation policies. These include "goods made to your specifications and/or clearly personalised".

In the same changes, Zalando also mentioned QR codes as an alternative to the classic return shipping label. Customers can download both options from their account.
