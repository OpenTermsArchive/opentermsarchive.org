# Policy on the use of AI

Open Terms Archive allows the use of AI tools in contributions. They do not, however, replace contributor responsibility or human judgment. This policy defines the conditions of use across [Open Terms Archive projects](https://github.com/OpenTermsArchive).

AI can be used to help write or suggest code and documentation, refactor or clarify existing work. Regardless of the form of the contribution, it must have been read and fully understood by its author before being submitted.

Contributions must not introduce license conflicts or reproduce third-party content. Ensuring the legal compliance of a submission is the contributor's responsibility.

Disclosing the use of AI is not required, but if a maintainer asks how a contribution was produced, an honest answer is expected.

If an Open Terms Archive project defines specific rules regarding the use of AI, those take precedence over this policy.
