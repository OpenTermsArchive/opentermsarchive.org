For users based in the European Economic Area or Switzerland, the data controller responsible for their information is Google Ireland Limited, unless otherwise stated in a service-specific privacy notice. ## Pinterest and Snapchat

Que **Pinterest** et **Snapchat** avaient retiré leurs mentions au Privacy Shield dans leurs conditions d’utilisation respectives. In a ruling handed down on Thursday, July 16, 2020 (Schrems II ruling), the EU’s top court ruled that this key data-sharing mechanism was invalid. The European Court of Justice more specifically said that the EU-US Privacy Shield failed to protect privacy and data protection rules. The decision has had immediate and complex implications for data sharing between the EU and US. See the changes here and there.
